Although the most eco-friendly way to stay covid safe is to wear reusable cloth masks, sometimes you may find yourself needing to use single-use masks. When disposing of them, make sure you cut the strings before tossing it in the trash so that wildlife like birds and small animals don’t get tangled in them.
