Our oceans will be flooded with an estimated 1.56 billion face masks in 2020, ... [resulting] in an additional 4,680 to 6,240 metric tonnes of marine plastic pollution... These masks will take as long as 450 years to break down, slowly turning into micro plastics while negatively impacting marine wildlife and ecosystems
