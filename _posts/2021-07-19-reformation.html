US women’s fashion brand, motto is “Being naked is the #1 most sustainable option. We’re #2”


1. Carbon neutral since 2015
2. Headquarters is powered by renewable sources like wind energy and solar panels
3. 75% of produced waste is recycled and their goal is to reach zero waste
4. Scraps of unused fabric are donated when possible, decreasing the company’s contribution to landfills
5. Cooperates with environmental agencies like Native Energy and Bonneville Environmental Foundation to support clean energy and water restoration initiatives
6. Packaging is either compostable or biodegradable
